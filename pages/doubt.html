Blood Donation Doubts

Donor Eligibility

  • What is the minimum age to donate blood?

    • You must be at least 18 years old to donate blood.

  • What is the minimum weight to donate blood?

    • You must weigh at least 50 kg to donate blood.

  • Can I donate blood if I have a medical condition?

    • It depends on the medical condition. Please contact us to determine your eligibility.

Blood Donation Process

  • What happens during the blood donation process?

    • Registration, medical history, blood test, donation, and refreshments.

  • How long does the blood donation process take?

    • Approximately 1-2 hours.

  • Will it hurt when I donate blood?

    • You may feel a slight pinch when the needle is inserted, but it's usually painless.

After Donation

  • What should i do after donating blood?

    • Rest,hydrate,and avoid heavy liifting or strenuous activities

  • Can I travel after donating blood?

    • it's recomended to avoid traveling for at least 24 hours after donating blood.

  • How long will i feel tired after donating blood?

    • You may tired for a few hours after donating blood .

Safety

  • Is blood donation safe?

    • Yes blood donation is a safe process

  • How do you prevent infection during blood donation?

    • We use sterile euipment,and our staff follow proper hygiene procedures.

  • What happen if i faint or feel dizzy during donation ?

    • Our trained staff will assist you and provide care until you feel better.
