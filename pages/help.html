About Blood Donation !

Blood donation is a voluntary procedure that can help save lives. There are several types of blood donation. Each type helps meet different medical needs.


Facts About Blood

# One unit of donated blood can save up to three lives. # You can donate blood every three months. It only takes 48 hours for your body fluids to be completely replenished. # Scientists have estimated the volume of blood in the human body to be eight percent of body weight.


Blood Doner criterias

  • Age = 18 years
  • Weight = 50 above
  • Normal blood pressure
  • Health = Doner must be good health
  • Hemoglobin = 12.5 grams per deciliter
